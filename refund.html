Refund Policy


This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds related to ICMBGSD 2025.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are applicable only for cancellations made within the stipulated time frame as mentioned during registration. Requests made beyond this period will not be entertained.

2. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow the steps below:

  • Email your refund request to icmbgsdcusat@gmail.com
  • Provide your registration details and proof of payment
  • Allow up to 14 business days for processing

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be provided in cases of no-shows or cancellations made after the conference start date.

4. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Refund Policy, please contact us at icmbgsdcusat@gmail.com.

This Refund Policy is effective as of DD/MM/2025.

ICMBGSD 25

The conference focuses on marine biodiversity, genomics, and sustainable practices, bringing experts together to discuss conservation strategies, cutting-edge research, and innovative solutions for preserving marine ecosystems.
