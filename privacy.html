Privacy Policy


This Privacy Policy outlines how information is collected, used, and protected on the ICMBGSD 2025 website.

1. Information Collection

We may collect personal information, including but not limited to names, email addresses, and affiliations, during the registration and abstract submission processes for ICMBGSD 2025. This information is used solely for conference-related purposes.

2. Information Usage

Collected information may be used for the following purposes:

  • Conference registration and communication
  • Abstract submission and review
  • Conference-related announcements and updates

3. Information Protection

We are committed to ensuring the security and confidentiality of collected information. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and appropriate measures are in place to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Links

The ICMBGSD 2025 website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of linked websites.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page. Users are advised to review the Privacy Policy periodically for updates.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at icmbgsdcusat@gmail.com.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of DD/MM/2025.
